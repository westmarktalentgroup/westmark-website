Luxury Residential Project Manager
Location: Austin, Texas

About Our Client:
Our client specializes in delivering extraordinary luxury residential projects that blend cutting-edge design, exceptional craftsmanship, and personalized service. Based in the vibrant city of Austin, Texas, they are committed to creating homes that reflect both timeless elegance and modern innovation.

Position Overview:
Our client is seeking an experienced and detail-oriented Luxury Residential Project Manager to lead high-end custom home builds and renovations across the Austin area. The ideal candidate will have a strong background managing upscale residential projects, coordinating with architects, designers, subcontractors, and clients to ensure flawless execution from concept through completion.

Key Responsibilities:

- Manage multiple luxury residential projects simultaneously with budgets ranging from $2M to $20M+
- Oversee project schedules, budgets, and quality control to ensure delivery on time and within budget
- Collaborate closely with architects, interior designers, engineers, and high-end subcontractors
- Serve as the primary client liaison, maintaining exceptional communication and delivering a white-glove experience
- Coordinate procurement of premium materials, finishes, and fixtures, ensuring alignment with design intent
- Lead regular site meetings and provide clear progress updates to all stakeholders
- Manage permitting, inspections, and compliance with local Austin building codes and regulations
- Identify risks proactively and develop solutions to keep projects on track
- Maintain thorough project documentation, including contracts, change orders, and RFIs

Qualifications:
- Minimum 7 years of experience managing luxury residential construction projects, preferably in Austin or similar markets
- Proven track record of successfully delivering high-end custom homes or renovations
- Strong knowledge of construction methods, materials, and quality standards for luxury homes
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to build trust with clients and partners
- Detail-oriented with outstanding organizational and problem-solving abilities
- Proficiency with project management software (Procore, Buildertrend, or similar)
- Understanding of Austin’s building codes, zoning, and permitting processes
- Ability to work independently and lead teams with confidence and professionalism
- Bachelor’s degree in Construction Management, Architecture, or related field preferred but not required
